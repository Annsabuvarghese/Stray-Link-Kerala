🐾 A Guide to Saving Lives

Every rescue matters. Follow these steps to ensure a safe and successful mission.

Step 1: Claim the Case

When you're ready to help, click "Claim". This prevents duplicate rescues and lets others know the case is in safe hands.

Step 2: Connect & Verify

Contact the reporter to confirm the exact location and current condition of the animal. Accuracy saves time — and lives.

Step 3: Reach Out for Support

You are never alone. Contact nearby volunteers, veterinarians, or adoption centers if you need help or guidance.

Step 4: Lead the Rescue

Move forward carefully. Prioritize your safety and the animal’s well-being at all times.

Step 5: Share the Good News

Upload rescue details to the site. After verification, the case will be officially marked as “Rescued.” 🎉

Step 6: Ensure Post-Rescue Care

Coordinate with a local vet or adoption center to begin medical care, recovery, and rehabilitation.

Step 7: Help Them Find a Forever Home

Keep the animal’s profile updated. Your updates help adopters discover their future best friend.

Friendly Reminder: If you’re unable to continue a claimed rescue, please unclaim the case immediately so another volunteer can step in without delay.
