The Spark Behind StrayLink

Walking through the vibrant streets of Kerala, we noticed a silent shadow over our state: the ignored struggle of our stray animals. We saw lives that needed a voice, wounds that needed healing, and souls that just wanted a home.

"We realized that the problem wasn't a lack of kindness, but a lack of connection."

StrayLink was built to be that bridge. We connect compassionate citizens like you directly with Government authorities, NGOs, Veterinary clinics, and dedicated volunteers.

Report

Found an animal in distress? Post a report with GPS coordinates so local heroes can find them instantly.

Rescue

NGOs and volunteers can claim cases, providing medical care and transport to safety.

🏠

Adopt

Once safe, animals are listed for adoption, turning a street struggle into a family fairy tale.

Thank you for being the change.

Their lives matter. With StrayLink, they are never alone again.
